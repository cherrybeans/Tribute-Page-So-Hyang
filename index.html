The beautiful and talented

So Hyang

The Outstanding Soprano Singer

So Hyang, full name Kim So-Hyang, is a Korean singer who has been in the music industry for years being only years. She is well known for her powerful voice and wide vocal range. So Hyang has participated many times on television music competition programs such as Immortal Songs 2 and I Am a Singer, winning twice on Immortal Songs 2 with songs "Lean On Me" and "Bridge Over Troubled Water". In 2017 she was on the television music competition show King of Masked Singer. She is very well known in Korea, though lesser known internationally. After winning Immortal Songs 2 with "Lean On Me" she dueted with Michael Bolton. She had her debut internationally in 2014 when she became the first Korean singer to sing the American national anthem at a NBA game.

Vocals

So Hyang has a very impressive vocal range which spans from D3 to A6: a total of 3 octaves, 3 notes, and a semitone. Her technique is wildly impressive as well. Her lower register is less developed, so she focuses on her higher tones which she excels at. She truly is a master at controlling her voice while making it look effortless, and she is proficient in several styles.
